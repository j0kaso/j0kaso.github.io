How to study dynamics?

Tools for interactive visualization | Molecular Dynamics

MD simulations: a computational microscope for molecular biology

Newton's equations of motion

F_i = m a_i
F_i = - ∂U_{total} / ∂r_i
U_{total} = U_{bonded} + U_{non-bonded}

F=force; m=mass; a=acceleration; -ðU=negative gradient of potential energy

How big are MDs?

 

Standard MD run:

  • ~ 80.000 atom coordinates
  • run for 0.5 µs
  • calculate coordinates each 2 fs
  • write coordinates each 10 ps

-> 40.000.000.000 coordinates

           = 40 GB

 

=> big data science
